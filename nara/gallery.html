Our Portfolio

Discover the beautiful transformations we've created

Classic Lashes - Natural & Elegant

Our signature classic lash extensions provide a natural and refined look. Each extension is carefully applied to individual natural lashes, creating a subtle enhancement perfect for daily wear and professional settings. The result is a timeless, elegant appearance.

Full Volume Set - Glamorous Look

For those who love drama and glamour, our volume lash extensions deliver stunning results. Multiple lightweight extensions are skillfully applied to create a full, voluminous appearance. Perfect for special occasions, events, or if you want to make a bold statement with your lashes.

Hybrid Extensions - Best of Both

Our hybrid technique combines the best of classic and volume methods, creating a uniquely textured and dimensional look. This style offers natural fullness with subtle drama, making it versatile for any occasion. A favorite among clients who appreciate balance and sophistication.

Colored Lashes - Fashion Forward

Stand out with our vibrant colored lash extensions. Available in various shades including classic black, deep burgundy, ocean blue, and rose gold. Mix and match colors to express your unique personality and style. Perfect for festivals, parties, or everyday fashion expression.

Dramatic Mega Volume - Show Stopper

Turn heads with our dramatic mega volume lash extensions. This intense style features ultra-full, fluffy lashes that make a powerful statement. Using premium lightweight materials, we create a show-stopping look that's comfortable to wear all day. Perfect for confident fashion lovers.

Subtle Enhancement - Everyday Glam

For those who prefer understated elegance, our subtle enhancement services add just the right amount of thickness and length to your natural lashes. This style is perfect for daily wear, work environments, and anyone who appreciates minimalist beauty with a touch of glamour.

Winged Design - Almond Eye Effect

Our winged lash design creates an almond eye effect that flatters any face shape. By strategically lengthening outer lashes, we create a lifted, youthful appearance. This style complements different eye shapes beautifully and creates a polished, put-together look every time.

Cat Eye Style - Bold & Edgy

Channel your inner fashion icon with our cat eye lash design. Longer outer lashes create the signature sharp, defined look that's bold and edgy. This dramatic style is perfect for date nights, parties, or whenever you want to command attention. A timeless favorite for lash enthusiasts.

Dolly Lashes - Wide-Eyed Wonder

Get that wide-eyed, innocent doll look with our dolly lash extension style. Longer lashes throughout create a wide-eye effect that's sweet and feminine. This style opens up the eyes and creates a youthful, approachable appearance. Perfect for adding a touch of whimsy to your look.

Ombre Lashes - Gradient Effect

Create a stunning gradient effect with our ombre lash extensions. Blending darker roots with lighter tips, this style adds depth and dimension to your lashes. The gradient effect is subtle yet eye-catching, creating a professional and artistic appearance.

Textured Lashes - Spiky Effect

Our textured lash style creates a spiky, choppy appearance that's edgy and fashion-forward. Varying lengths throughout create movement and dimension, giving your lashes a trendy, Instagram-worthy look. Perfect for those who want something unique and eye-catching.

Party Lashes - Special Occasion

Celebrate in style with our party lash extensions, designed to turn heads at events. We combine dramatic volume, length, and sometimes glitter accents for maximum impact. Perfect for weddings, galas, festivals, and any special occasion where you want to look absolutely stunning.

Mega Glam - Red Carpet Ready

Feel like a celebrity with our mega glam lash extensions. This ultra-luxurious style features maximum volume, length, and drama. Using our finest materials, we create lashes that photograph beautifully and make you feel like you're walking a red carpet. For those who aren't afraid to shine!
