About Nara Lashes

Our Story

Nara Lashes was founded with a simple mission: to provide premium eyelash extension services that enhance natural beauty while maintaining the health and integrity of natural lashes. With years of experience in the beauty industry, our founder recognized a gap in the market for professional, certified lash artists who combine technical expertise with genuine care for their clients.

Today, Nara Lashes stands as a beacon of excellence in the lash extension industry. We've proudly served thousands of satisfied clients across Nairobi, each leaving our studio with beautiful, confidence-boosting lashes and a desire to return.

Our Mission

We are committed to delivering exceptional lash extension services that transform our clients' confidence and beauty. Our mission is to:

  • Provide professional, certified lash extension services using premium materials
  • Ensure the health and safety of our clients' natural lashes
  • Create customized lash designs that complement each client's unique features
  • Maintain the highest standards of hygiene and professionalism
  • Build lasting relationships with our clients based on trust and exceptional service
  • Continuously train and develop our team to stay at the forefront of lash artistry
  • Make premium lash services accessible with competitive pricing

Why Choose Us?

At Nara Lashes, we go beyond just applying lashes. We're dedicated to creating an experience that leaves you feeling beautiful, confident, and valued. Here's what sets us apart:

  • Certified Professionals: All our lash artists are certified and trained in the latest techniques
  • Premium Materials: We use only the finest quality adhesives and synthetic lashes
  • Customized Service: Every lash design is tailored to your eye shape, preferences, and lifestyle
  • Hygiene First: We maintain strict hygiene standards with sterilized tools and clean workspaces
  • Competitive Pricing: Premium quality at prices that respect your budget
  • Friendly Team: We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming, comfortable environment
  • Aftercare Support: We provide detailed care instructions and are always available for questions

Our Team

Our team is composed of dedicated, certified lash extension artists who are passionate about beauty and customer service. Each team member brings their own expertise and artistic flair to create stunning lash designs. We believe in continuous learning and regularly update our skills with the latest techniques and trends in the lash industry. When you visit Nara Lashes, you're not just getting a service—you're getting the benefit of our team's collective expertise and genuine care for your beauty journey.

Quality & Safety Standards

At Nara Lashes, quality and safety are non-negotiable. We adhere to strict standards including:

  • All tools and equipment are sterilized between clients
  • We use hypoallergenic, medical-grade adhesives
  • Our lash extensions are made from premium synthetic materials
  • All artists follow strict application protocols to protect natural lashes
  • We maintain detailed client records for allergy tracking and service history
  • Regular training on health and safety regulations
